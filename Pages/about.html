About Whiskers Café

Whiskers Café opened its doors in 2019 with a simple idea: that great coffee and the calming presence of cats could make the world a little better. Founded by animal lovers Sophie and James, the café grew out of a desire to create a truly welcoming space for everyone — from busy students needing a study break to families looking for a memorable afternoon out.

Our cats are central to everything we do. Every feline at Whiskers either lives here as a permanent resident or is with us while they wait for their forever home through our charity partner, Manchester Cat Rescue. When you visit, you're not just having a coffee, you're helping us give our cats the socialisation they need to thrive.

We're proud to have helped rehome over 140 cats since we opened. Our cats always come first: they have access to quiet areas away from guests, and our trained staff ensure they're happy and healthy every single day.

We source all our food and drink from local, independent suppliers in Greater Manchester. Our menu changes with the seasons, and we cater for a wide range of dietary requirements, just ask at the counter.

Animal Care & Café Rules

Be Gentle

Always approach cats calmly and let them come to you first. Never force interaction with a cat that is resting or moving away.

No Human Food

Please do not feed any café food or drink to our cats. Human food can be harmful to them. Our staff provide their food on a schedule.

Respect Rest Time

If a cat is sleeping or in a raised rest area, please do not disturb them. Rest is essential for their health and happiness.

Supervise Children

Children under 12 must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times. Please guide young visitors on how to interact kindly.

Keep Noise Low

Our cats enjoy a calm atmosphere. Please keep voices low and avoid sudden loud noises that may startle or stress them.

Photography

You're welcome to photograph our cats — just no flash photography. It can startle and distress animals. Natural light works beautifully anyway!
