Welcome to Whiskers Café

Whiskers Café is Manchester's cosiest cat café – a place where great food, relaxation, and the warmth of feline company come together under one roof.

Enjoy freshly baked cakes, artisan coffee, and loose-leaf teas while our resident cats stroll, stretch, and snooze around you. Whether you're after a peaceful afternoon away from the city buzz or a unique experience with friends, our café has something for everyone.

Many of our cats are also available for adoption. Fall in love over a latte, and you might just find your perfect companion. Every adoption goes through our trusted charity partner.

Cat of the Week

🌟 Meet Marmalade

Marmalade is a cheerful three-year-old black and white tabby who loves nothing more than watching the world from a sunny windowsill. He's known for his sky-high purr volume and his habit of gently head-butting visitors he takes a shine to.

Personality traits:

  • Friendly
  • Playful
  • Affectionate
  • Curious
  • Great with children

Marmalade is currently available for adoption and is looking for a loving home with plenty of cosy spots and sunny windows.

Available for Adoption
Marmalade has been with us for six months and has charmed every single visitor. He's neutered, vaccinated, and microchipped – ready to come home with you. Ask a member of staff today or visit our Our Cats page to start the adoption process.
